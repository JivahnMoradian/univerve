About Univerve
Univerve is a boutique consulting and strategic advisory firm, with a global perspective. We provide a variety of services, including restructuring and interim management, M&A advisory, hive-offs, JVs and alliances, strategy advising, and consulting. We specialize in helping clients in Europe and in the U.S.A. who seek to do business in India, or with Indian firms who seek to do business abroad.
Univerve has a team of specialized and accomplished associates, who are selectively brought in to meet our clients' specific needs, and actively participate in all stages of a project. Examples of expertise inlcude domain knowledge of IT, technical knowledge of chemical processes (e.g. coal liquefaction), local knowledge of an acquisition target, and experience dealing with local government rules and regulations. We pride ourselves on coaching and guiding our clients throughout the process, and continue to support them until the end, regardless of the outcome.
